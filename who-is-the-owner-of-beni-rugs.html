Commitment to Sustainable Sourcing

Beni Rugs prioritizes environmentally friendly practices at every stage of production. Sourcing materials sustainably is crucial for the brand, focusing on natural fibers such as wool and cotton that are renewable and biodegradable. This commitment not only helps minimize ecological impact but also supports traditional, artisanal techniques that have been passed down through generations.

Transparency is another crucial element of the company's sourcing approach. Beni Rugs works closely with artisans in Morocco, ensuring that fair wages and ethical labor practices are upheld. By fostering direct relationships with producers, the brand can maintain quality while promoting social responsibility within the communities involved in the weaving process.
